Godzilla Attack

concept

Players play as godzilla in a top down world and try to destroy all the building in the city. Inspiration taken from the game Rampage.

story

Godzilla wants to go back home but there is a city in his way. Take control of Godzilla and help him to destroy the city and get back home safely! Don't spare a life!

gameplay

Players will take control of Godzilla and try to destroy all the buildings in the city.
The player will be attacked by helicopters and tanks and Godzilla must destroy them as well to be able to proceed safely.
Eventually, you are going to bring Godzilla to his cave and finish the level.
Occasionally power-ups will drop to enhance Godzilla’s abilities such as atomic breath or harder scales.
Atomic breath will destroy everything within a certain range in front of Godzilla and harder scales will increase the player’s health.

controls

Players will use the arrow keys to move and the space bar to attack. If the player has power-ups then they can use the power-up with Q, W and E keys.

ui

The user interface will be fairly clean, the only things on the UI will be Godzilla’s health and number of buildings left to destroy.
